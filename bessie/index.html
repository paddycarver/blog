Your Data. Not Ours.

We don't want your data, we want to help you make use of it. Our privacy policy is simple: "do not surprise the user." We try to be up-front about any data we collect, and we hope if you have questions you'll get in touch.

Nobody likes being spied on. Our solution is simple: we don't spy on you.

Tap. Share. Anywhere.

We are slowly but surely supporting new devices and clients. We want to give you a native, seamless experience wherever you get your content and wherever you want to see it.

Building an awesome, native, and intuitive experience takes time. Please be patient as we work to support your preferred platform.

Sharing is Caring.

We like to think that Second Bit has a pretty awesome team, but we're not perfect. Fortunately, we don't have to be: 2cloud is completely, 100% open-source software.

If we're not doing something as well as we could be, or if you have a great idea for a new feature, let us know. We like when users file issues. We love when users send pull requests.
