About 2cloud

We're here for you.

When we were building 2cloud, we took care to make it open. We wanted something that would place the user above all other priorities.

The Who

2cloud is open source software. Anyone can contribute to it.

The development is sponsored and driven by Second Bit, whose founders created the project. Our employees are passionate users of the web, and we try to use that to shape how we build for the web. We continuously strive to build things that we would like to use. See our about page for more information.

The What

2cloud is the sweet spot between a messaging platform and a bookmarking service. It allows you to open links, instantly, on your devices from any of your other devices.

What differentiates 2cloud from services like Instapaper is its focus on devices. We don't want to just store a list of things you'd like to look at when you get the chance; we want to empower you to control when and where you access your content.

The Why

It is no secret that 2cloud began as a learning experiment. Since then, it has grown into an international tool covered by some of the greatest publications in the field.

Despite this, 2cloud remains true to its nature: it is all about the user. Our mission is to return control of the web browsing experience to the user. You should be able to view your content on whichever device is most appropriate or convenient, and on your own time. By making the process of moving content from one device to another simple, 2cloud promotes the power of the user to choose how they consume content.
