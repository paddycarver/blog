Ground Sail Windmills by Dennis Jarvis

Tilting at Windmills

My name’s Paddy. I live in Pasco, Washington with my husband and our puppy. I write software, give talks, and inflict my opinions on anyone who will listen.

I started the gets by project because I think mental illness is something we should talk about.

I wrote Your API Is Bad because I have a lot of feels about API design, and enough people listened to me about them that I figured it made sense to put them in a book. It’s a work in progress, and released under a Creative Commons license. You can buy it from me at whatever price you like.

I’ve been told that I’m overly idealistic and have been accused of being an impractical zealot. I’m at peace with these designations.
